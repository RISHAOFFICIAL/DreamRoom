# The Sanctuary Pack: Soundscape Specification

## Atmosphere
A deep, low-frequency atmospheric drone that feels like the quiet hum of a stone temple. It provides a "floor" for the auditory experience, evoking a sense of safety and containment.

## Instrumentation
- **Shakuhachi Fragments**: Subtle, non-resolving melodic lines from a Japanese wooden flute. These should feel spontaneous and airy.
- **Crystalline Chimes**: High-frequency, sharp but delicate chimes that occur at randomized intervals. They act as "light" in the soundscape.
- **Vellum Textures**: A very faint "rustle" sound that mimics the touch of high-quality paper, reinforcing the tactile nature of the DreamRoom.

## Natural Elements
- **Filtered Water**: The sound of a mountain stream, processed through a low-pass filter to remove harshness. It should sound "warm" and constant.
- **Distant Forest**: Extremely faint bird calls (reverberated) that give a sense of vast outdoor space beyond the "sanctuary."

## Emotional Quality
Grounded, spacious, and safe. The soundscape is designed to lower the heart rate of the user and encourage a state of "Witnessing" and deep intention.

## Usage Triggers
- **Ambient**: Loops while the user is editing a board with the Sanctuary theme active.
- **Selection**: A specific "shimmer" sound when a Sanctuary asset is selected from the shop.
